Feature Breakdown

Below is a simple explanation of what each main feature will do and how it will help the business in practice. Features marked as Premium will belong to the EUR 270 package.

Core

Website

The website will be the public face of the business. It will explain the bootcamp clearly, look professional, and help new people trust the brand.

Example: someone finds the business online, understands the offer, sees the value clearly, and feels confident contacting or booking.

Core

Client Accounts

Each client will be able to have their own private account inside the app, where they can see the information that belongs to them.

Example: a client logs in and checks their schedule, profile, private sessions, progress, and messages in one place.

Core

Availability

You will be able to choose which weeks are available, how many places exist, and whether those weeks are open or closed.

Example: if a week is full or not active, it will stop being offered as available for booking.

Core

Bookings

The booking system will let the business manage 1-week, 2-week, and 3-week stays in an organized way, connected to real availability.

Example: if someone books 2 consecutive weeks, those places will be counted automatically in the relevant weeks.

Core

Schedule

The platform will include a schedule system so clients can see their on-site plan while they are in the bootcamp.

Example: the client can open their dashboard and see meals, training, and activities without staff having to send everything manually.

Premium

Coach and Nutritionist Access

The premium package will include separate access for coach and nutritionist, so the team can work inside the platform with more structure while admin keeps full control.

Example: the coach can update the training side of the schedule, and the nutritionist can update the food side, all inside one system.

Core

Private Sessions

The platform will also allow follow-up private sessions outside the normal bootcamp stay, such as online coaching or nutrition check-ins.

Example: a client can have a Zoom or WhatsApp follow-up session after the bootcamp, without mixing that into the normal on-site schedule.

Premium

Messaging

The premium package will include private messaging inside the platform, so clients and staff can communicate in a more organized way.

Example: a client can ask about a private session or a schedule detail directly inside the app instead of depending on scattered messages.

Premium

Push Notifications

The premium package will also include push notifications, which means the system can send alerts directly to the user when something important happens.

Example: a client could receive a notification saying they have a new message, a booking update, or a change in their schedule.

Core

Reports and Exports

Admin will be able to export important business information, making it easier to review client data and booking/payment numbers.

Example: you will be able to download a client database or a bookings/payments report to work with the numbers more easily.

Core

Payments

The project is also being prepared for payment flows, so the business can eventually manage deposits or payments inside the ecosystem.

Example: in the future, a client could move from seeing the offer to completing a payment without leaving the business system.
