These references are not exact copies of this project, but they show the general pricing environment around custom software, websites, and maintenance.

Current References

Scenic IT Solutions - Custom web app development cost (2026)

This source shows a mid-sized custom application range of $10,000 - $40,000, and places more complex software above that.

https://www.scenicitsolutions.com/blogs/cost-of-custom-web-app-development-2026.html
Arc - Full-stack developer hourly rate

Arc shows full-stack freelance developers commonly averaging around $61-80/hour. That is useful context for understanding how custom platform work is normally valued.

https://arc.dev/freelance-developer-rates/full-stack
WebFX - Website maintenance pricing (2026)

WebFX shows that maintenance is usually a real ongoing cost. Their page references web application maintenance around $300 - $2,500/month, and custom business website maintenance around $350 - $4,500/month, depending on complexity.

https://www.webfx.com/web-development/pricing/website-maintenance/
WebFX - Website build cost (2026)

WebFX also shows business websites commonly ranging from $1,000 - $100,000 depending on size, design, and functionality. This helps explain why a custom site plus private app is not in the same category as a basic brochure website.

https://www.webfx.com/web-design/pricing/website-costs/
ZTABS - SaaS development cost breakdown (2026)

ZTABS shows how larger software products can scale much higher, with MVP development often starting well above small website budgets. It also highlights that hosting, support, and post-launch work are real ongoing costs.

https://ztabs.co/blog/saas-development-cost-breakdown
This reference sheet is meant to give context, not to claim that every agency would quote the exact same amount. The point is simple: this type of project sits in the custom software category, not in the simple website category.
